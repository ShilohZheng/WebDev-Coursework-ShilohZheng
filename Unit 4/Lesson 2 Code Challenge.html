How to Make Doubles:

  1. Mix flour and water to make dough
  2. Form small circles and fry until puffy
  3. Prepare curried chickpea filling
  4. Assemble with tamarind sauce

Featured Recipe: Doubles

My grandmother's famous doubles - fried dough with curched chickpeas and tamarind sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • Trinidad curry powder
  • Tamarind paste

Trinidadian Cooking Terms:

Doubles
A popular street food with two pieces of fried bread
Roti
Flatbread used to wrap curried dishes

Recipe Variations:

  • Spice Levels:
    • Mild (default)
    • Medium (add pepper sauce)
    • Hot (extra scotch bonnet)
  • Toppings:
    • Classic tamarind
    • Cucumber chutney
